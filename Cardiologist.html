About Cardiologists

Cardiologists are specialized doctors who diagnose and treat diseases of the heart and blood vessels. They use advanced tools and tests to manage cardiovascular conditions, improve heart function, and prevent future complications.

1. Skills & Technologies

Interpreting heart rhythms and electrical activity to detect abnormalities using ECG machines.
Using ultrasound to assess heart structure, valve function, and blood flow dynamics.
Inserting catheters into the heart to diagnose or treat blockages and other conditions.
Monitoring and treating high blood pressure to reduce cardiovascular risks.
Performing stent placement, angioplasty, and minimally invasive repairs to restore blood flow.
Advising lifestyle changes and medication to prevent heart disease before symptoms arise.
