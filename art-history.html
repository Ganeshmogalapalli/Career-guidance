The Journey of Art Through Time

1. Prehistoric Art

Dating back over 40,000 years, prehistoric art includes cave paintings, carvings, and stone structures. These works provide insight into early human life and spirituality.

2. Classical Antiquity

The art of ancient Greece and Rome focused on realism, human anatomy, and harmony. Iconic works include sculptures of gods, heroes, and architectural marvels like the Parthenon.

3. The Renaissance

A period of rebirth in art, science, and culture during the 14th–17th centuries. Artists like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo revolutionized techniques in painting and sculpture.

4. Modern Art

Spanning from the 19th to 20th centuries, movements like Impressionism, Cubism, and Surrealism challenged conventions. Notable figures include Van Gogh, Picasso, and Dali.

5. Contemporary Art

Today’s art reflects globalism, digital technology, and social commentary. It spans mediums from traditional painting to installations and digital media.
