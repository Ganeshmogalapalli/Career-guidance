About Berklee Online

Berklee Online is the online extension school of Berklee College of Music, providing access to world-class music education for students around the globe. It offers a range of certificate, bachelor’s, and master’s degree programs in music and the music business.

Popular Courses

Music Production

Learn how to record, mix, and master music using professional tools and techniques taught by industry experts.

Songwriting

Master the art of songwriting from melody and harmony to lyrics and arrangement with guidance from professional songwriters.

Guitar Techniques

Explore different guitar styles, improve technique, and develop improvisation skills with Berklee’s guitar faculty.

Music Business

Understand how the music industry works and learn to manage and promote music in the modern digital landscape.
