Cleveland Clinic

Cleveland Clinic is one of the largest and most respected hospitals in the U.S., particularly known for heart surgery and cardiac care. Its commitment to innovation and patient outcomes has made it a top choice globally.

It offers care across more than 140 medical specialties and subspecialties.

Company Overview

  1. Founded: 1921
  2. Headquarters: Cleveland, Ohio, USA
  3. Type: Non-profit Academic Medical Center
  4. Global Presence: Ohio, Florida, Nevada, Canada, Abu Dhabi, and London
  5. Specialties: Cardiology, Oncology, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Surgery, and more
  6. Employees: Over 77,000 (2023)

Employee Packages & Benefits

  1. Health Insurance: Medical, dental, vision
  2. Retirement Plans: 403(b) with employer match
  3. PTO: Generous paid time off and holidays
  4. Education: Tuition reimbursement, ongoing training
  5. Wellness: Physical & mental health programs
  6. Perks: Discounts, relocation aid, career growth
