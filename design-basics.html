1. Principles of Design

Design is built on principles such as balance, contrast, emphasis, movement, proportion, and unity. These guide the composition and ensure harmony in visuals.

2. Typography

Typography is the art of arranging type. Knowing how to choose fonts, adjust spacing, and use hierarchy is key to communicating effectively through text.

3. Color Theory

Colors evoke emotion and guide user attention. Understanding complementary, analogous, and triadic schemes helps create visual impact.

4. Composition

Grids, alignment, white space, and the rule of thirds are essential tools to structure visual content and make designs aesthetically pleasing.

5. Tools for Design

Software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and Canva help bring creative ideas to life with a wide array of digital design features.
