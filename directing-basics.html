Introduction to Directing

Directing is both an art and a craft. It involves storytelling, leadership, communication, and vision. This page covers the foundational principles needed to step into the role of a director.

Core Concepts

Visual Storytelling

Learn how to communicate through framing, lighting, and camera movement.

Working with Actors

Understand how to guide performances and create emotional impact.

Script Analysis

Break down scenes, arcs, and beats to understand the heart of a story.

Pre-Production Planning

Develop shot lists, storyboards, and scheduling techniques for efficient shooting.
