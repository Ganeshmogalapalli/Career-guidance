Understanding Music Theory

Music theory is the study of the practices and possibilities of music. It is the language musicians use to understand and communicate musical structure, scales, harmony, rhythm, and more.

Key Concepts

Scales and Modes

Major and minor scales form the basis of Western music. Modes like Dorian and Lydian offer alternative tonalities and textures.

Intervals and Chords

Understanding the distance between notes (intervals) and how chords are constructed is essential to harmony and melody.

Rhythm and Meter

Rhythm defines the timing of music. Time signatures and beat patterns help organize music into understandable structures.

Harmony and Voice Leading

Harmony adds depth to music, and voice leading ensures smooth transitions between chords and musical lines.
